# 🌙 Najma Prayer Widget - Setup Guide for Workers

The Prayer Widget is designed for workers who want subtle prayer time awareness while at their desk.

## 🎯 Access the Widget

Navigate to: **`#/prayer-widget`** in your Najma app

Example: If your Najma app is at `https://yourdomain.com`, go to:
```
https://yourdomain.com#/prayer-widget
```

---

## 📌 Step 1: Pin the Tab (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED)

### Chrome:
1. Right-click on the Prayer Widget tab
2. Click **"Pin Tab"**
3. The tab shrinks to just show 🌙 icon and prayer countdown

### Safari:
1. Right-click on the Prayer Widget tab
2. Click **"Pin Tab"**
3. Tab moves to the left sidebar

### Firefox:
1. Right-click on the Prayer Widget tab
2. Click **"Pin Tab"**

**Result:** The tab stays tiny, always visible, and shows:
- 🌙 icon
- Next prayer name
- Countdown timer in the tab title

Example tab title: `🌙 Asr in 1:23:45`

---

## 🔔 Step 2: Enable Notifications (Optional but Recommended)

1. Click **"Enable Notifications"** in the widget settings
2. Allow notifications when your browser prompts you
3. You'll receive alerts:
   - **15 minutes** before iqama
   - **5 minutes** before iqama
   - **At iqama time** (persistent notification)

---

## 🕌 Step 3: Select Your Masjid

1. First time: Widget will ask you to select a masjid
2. Choose from the list of available masajid
3. Your selection is saved automatically
4. Change anytime by clicking the **Settings (⚙️)** icon

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Maximum Productivity:
1. **Pin the tab** - Keeps it always visible without taking space
2. **Enable notifications** - Get alerts without watching the clock
3. **Position the pinned tab** - Drag it to the leftmost position for easy visibility
4. **Check it at a glance** - The tab title shows time remaining even when minimized

### Battery Saving:
- The widget uses minimal resources
- Updates only once per second (very lightweight)
- Notifications are browser-native (no extra background processes)

### Multi-Monitor Setup:
- Keep the widget tab on your secondary monitor
- Or keep it pinned on your main browser window
- Notifications appear regardless of which monitor you're on

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Notifications Not Working?
1. Check browser notification permissions:
   - Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Site Settings → Notifications
   - Safari: Safari → Preferences → Websites → Notifications
2. Make sure your OS allows browser notifications
3. Try clicking "Enable Notifications" again

### Tab Title Not Updating?
1. Refresh the page
2. Make sure you've selected a masjid
3. Check that the page hasn't crashed (try clicking on it)

### Wrong Prayer Times?
1. Go to Settings (⚙️)
2. Select the correct masjid
3. Times sync from the main Najma database
